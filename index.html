About us

About Us

Since 2016, Masters Contracting has been a premier roofing company serving homeowners around the Saint Louis area. As a family-owned, manufacturer-certified, and customer-focused team, we deliver a trifecta of value you won’t find anywhere else! From replacement roofs to roof repairs, our team brings years of combined experience to guarantee a job done right in every home, every time.

  • Storm Damage Specialist

    We are storm damage and inspection experts. After experiencing hail, wind, and other natural disasters, Roofing Force will take care of all of your insurance claim needs (including roofing, gutters, siding, windows and interior damage work, etc.). If we find proof of storm damage, then we will provide you with a comprehesive damage report that you can submit to your insurance company.

  • Best Roofing Solutions

    Unlike most roofers that only install asphalt shingles, we understand all types of roofing products. Our state-of-the-art solutions include architectural shingles, stone coated steel, composite, concrete, and metal panel roofing products that last much longer, provide better storm protection, and energy efficiencies than traditional asphalt shingles and are guaranteed to improve the curb appeal of your home.

  • Add Value And Protection To Your Home

    After we replace your roof and fix the other storm damage to your house, your home’s resale value will increase. Our roofing solutions will help to prevent future storm damage and may save you money due to increased energy efficiencies and insurance discounts. In most cases, your insurance premiums should not go up after you file a claim because storm damage is an "act of nature".

  • Quality Craftsmanship

    We deliver the best service, materials and value on every job. No installation is too big or complicated for us. If you have a roofing or other storm damage problem, then Roofing Force will fix it.

  • Workmanship And Material Warranties

    We provide some of the best workmanship & materials warranties in the industry. We are licensed and insured so you can rest assured.

FREE ROOF INSPECTION!

If it's been more than five years since your roof was last inspected, or you have reason to believe that it needs attention, call Masters Contracting for a free inspection and a no-obligation free estimate on any repairs that may be needed. We will send a factory-certified roofing inspector to carry out a thorough 17-point inspection.
